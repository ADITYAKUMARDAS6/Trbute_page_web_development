CRISTIANO RONALDO (G.O.A.T)

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro, commonly known as Cristiano Ronaldo, is a Portuguese professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players in the history of the sport. Born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal, Ronaldo has achieved extraordinary success throughout his career
